A 3-week user experience exercise into making PRESTO the go-to commuter’s app for daily transit planning
THE PROBLEM: Transit commuters are often frustrated when planning and taking their trips because they use multiple apps at once when doing so.

THE SOLUTION: By putting essential trip planning features into a complete package, our new PRESTO app is designed to make the transit planning process more efficient and easy to use.
In the development of our user plan, we wanted to ensure that we perform tests to discover the problems users may have when transit app and the Presto app in its current form. Our objectives are 1) to understand past and present experiences with transit commuting, 2) understand the kind of services interviewees currently use for commuting and 3) the sort of issues or problems users face during their commute.

The Presto app as designed today is deficient and does not function or provide users with the necessary usability to perform daily tasks.

We wanted it to feel familiar,  but allow users to easily navigate and understand new features as they find their way through the app. In our final round of user testing, we were able to ascertain that the flow, fit and feel of the new features were purposeful.
